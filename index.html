Ornaments have been oppressed ever since the birth of Modernism. Thus, their rich history and narrative on human have been lost. Now modernism is a masculine force constantly being utilized all over the world as a westernizing agency.
Ornaments’ pure formal qualities and irrationality are what make us human.
The advancement in technology helps us to create finest details with ease. New things are possible too like making ornaments that move.
For my degree project I am building a library of digital ornaments and I need your help. Please send one or more ornamental forms to me and I will feature them in the final website. The form could be anything – from a single rule to elaborate drawings. Also you can draw them however you like and I will vectorize them for you. If you want your ornaments to change its shape, please send starting and ending forms so that I can animate them. Please send your submission to yryoo@risd.edu.
